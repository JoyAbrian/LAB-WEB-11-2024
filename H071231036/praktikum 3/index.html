Today we are one of the largest producers of iron ore and nickel in the world, in addition to operating in other segments. Come and discover each product and its curiosities:

At PT Vale Indonesia, mining is at the core of what we do. We focus on responsible nickel extraction from our operations in Sulawesi, utilizing sustainable practices to meet the growing global demand for nickel, a key resource for electric vehicle batteries and renewable energy technologies. Our commitment to Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) principles ensures that we minimize environmental impact, support local communities, and contribute to Indonesia’s transition towards a greener economy. By collaborating with industry partners and stakeholders, we are driving innovation in mining while fostering long-term growth and sustainability for Indonesia​
